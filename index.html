Building Youth Employability Through Skills (BYETS)

About Us

Swisscontact is implementing the BYETS project from 2022-2026 with the support of Euro 4 million from the Embassy of the Kingdom of the Netherlands. The project envisions increasing income of the youth by creating decent employment and self-employment opportunities across three sectors and four divisions. The project will address the existing constraints in developing the skills of the youth and support to establish a systematic approach to provide market-driven training specifically by:

a. Building the capacity of Training Service Providers (TSPs) to design and implement quality training and job placement support services.
